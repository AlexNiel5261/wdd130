Overview

Purpose

To gather, compare, and inform various space launch providers and their various vehicles, engines, methods, history, etc. Used Launch Providers: SpaceX, Blue Origin, RocketLab, ULA, NASA*, ArianeSpace

* Orginization is government owned rather than corporate.

Audience

Fellow Space Enthusiasts
